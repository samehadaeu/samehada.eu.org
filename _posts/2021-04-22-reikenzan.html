Released: January 09, 2016
Runtime: 24 mins
Genres: Comedy, Fantasy

 Reikenzan: Hoshikuzu-tachi no Utage: In the anime's story, a comet falling will bring about a great calamity, and in order to choose a child fated by the falling comet, the family of the "Reiken" clan with a long storied history is once again resuming its entrance examination process to find disciples. The protagonist, who possesses a special soul that is only available once in a thousand years, decides to take the exam, and he starts down the path toward becoming an exceptional sage.
